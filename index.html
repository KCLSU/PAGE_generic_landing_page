The Disabled Students' Network

This Network refers to all students who self-define as disabled, including (if they wish) but not limited to, students with a physical or mental impairment, a long-term medical or mental health condition or Specific Learning Difficulties (SpLDs). Identify as Disabled? Check our page for more specific updates .

You can also contact disabled@kclsu.org about taking part, or email representation@kclsu.org for more information about networks.
